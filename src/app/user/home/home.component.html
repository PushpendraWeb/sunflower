About Us

Welcome to Sunflower Health Plus, where exceptional nephrology care is delivered by the renowned Dr. Jayesh Prabhakar Waghulde, an MD, DM-qualified Professor of Nephrology with over 23 years of expertise. Our clinic focuses on specialized areas such as nephrology, dialysis, kidney transplant, transplant failure, and graft dysfunction. Dr. Waghulde’s commitment ensures cutting-edge treatments and compassionate care, with a team of professionals dedicated to the highest standards of excellence.

At Sunflower Health Plus, we prioritize personalized and comprehensive care plans tailored to individual patient needs. We offer advanced treatments that balance medical excellence with patient comfort, utilizing state-of-the-art technologies in nephrology. Whether it’s managing kidney diseases through medication and lifestyle adjustments, providing advanced dialysis services including hemodialysis and peritoneal dialysis, or overseeing kidney transplant procedures, Sunflower Health Plus offers a holistic approach to healthcare. For those undergoing kidney transplants, our clinic provides comprehensive pre-transplant evaluation, meticulous post-transplant care, and personalized immunosuppressive regimens to minimize the risk of rejection and maximize the success of the transplant.

Our mission is to be a centre for excellence in Nephrology. We offer not only top-notch medical care but also empathetic support throughout the healthcare journey.
